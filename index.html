Our Treatments

Heal Here

General Ortho

Experiencing joint pain or discomfort? We specialize in a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions, from birth-related issues to age-related degeneration. We offer comprehensive diagnosis and treatment plans to help you regain mobility and improve your quality of life.

Knee Pain

Knee pain can significantly impact your daily activities. Dr Jaya Prasad provides specialized care for various knee conditions, including injuries, and arthritis. Our goal is to alleviate pain, restore function, and help you get back to doing what you love.

Shoulder

Shoulder pain can limit your range of motion and hinder your daily activities. We offer advanced treatments for injuries, rotator cuff tears, arthritis, and other shoulder conditions. We focus on providing effective solutions to restore shoulder function and reduce pain.

Foot and Ankle

The complex structure of the foot and ankle makes them prone to various injuries and conditions. Our experts diagnose and treat a wide range of foot and ankle problems, including sprains, fractures, arthritis, and diabetic foot ulcers. We offer personalized treatment plans to help you maintain foot and ankle health.

Hip

Hip pain can interfere with your mobility and overall well-being. We provide comprehensive care for hip conditions, including arthritis, hip dysplasia, and hip fractures.

Hand, Wrist, and Elbow

Injuries to the hand, wrist, and elbow can significantly impact your daily activities. Our specialists offer expert diagnosis and treatment for a variety of conditions, including carpal tunnel syndrome, tendonitis, fractures, and arthritis. We focus on restoring hand and upper extremity function.

Our Expertise

Sports Medicine

Dr. Jaya Prasad offers comprehensive care for athletic injuries. This includes prevention, diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation to optimize athletic performance and longevity.

Sports Rehabilitation

Specialized programs designed to restore function, strength, and flexibility post-injury. Dr. Jaya Prasad provides individualized plans to facilitate a safe and expeditious return to sports.

Arthroscopy

A minimally invasive surgical technique utilizing a small camera to visualize and treat joint issues. Dr. Jaya Prasad employs arthroscopy for precise diagnosis and treatment of various joint conditions.

Hip Replacement

Surgical procedure to replace a damaged hip joint with an artificial implant. Dr. Jaya Prasad performs hip replacements to alleviate pain and restore mobility in patients with degenerative hip conditions.

Total Hip Replacement

Complete replacement of the femoral head and acetabulum with prosthetic components. Dr. Jaya Prasad specializes in total hip replacements for advanced arthritis or joint damage.

ACL Joint

The Anterior Cruciate Ligament (ACL) is a crucial knee stabilizer. Dr. Jaya Prasad offers comprehensive ACL treatment, including non-surgical and surgical options, followed by rigorous rehabilitation.
